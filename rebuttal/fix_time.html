Note that the cell highlighted in red indicates the ranking difference; under 9-hour threshold; e.g., the fix time of cant.resolve is longer than the fix time of incompatible.types, which is different under the 12-hour threshold.

Specifically, comparing 12-hour threshold with 9-hour threshold, we find that the fix time of most error types has no significant difference. Comparing 12-hour threshold with 6-hour threshold, we find that the only significant difference is the fix time of method.does.not.override.superclass, decreased from 74 minites to 35 minites. We will add this sensitivity analysis in the paper.
